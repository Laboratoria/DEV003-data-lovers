What is it and how does pokemon go work?

It is an augmented reality video game. Internet connection is required to play, it is a free game but it contains microtransactions. The game consists of finding and capturing characters from the Pokémon saga hidden in real-world locations and fighting with them, which involves physically moving through the streets of the city to progress. complete your Pokédex, strengthen yourself and battle online with other trainers during PvP Raid combat.
